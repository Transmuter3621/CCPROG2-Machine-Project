Lasagna
4 main
Ingredients 10
200.000 grams lasagna sheets
300.000 grams tomato sauce
150.000 milliters olive oil
300.000 grams ground beef
300.000 grams ground pork
10.000 grams salt
10.000 grams pepper
48.000 grams garlic
48.000 grams onion
100.000 grams mozzarella cheese
Steps 7
Boil lasagna sheets for 10 minutes
Mix ground beef and pork with garlic and onion
Sautee the meat in olive oil
Mix in tomato sauce
Season with salt and pepper
Layer the sheets, meat mixture, and cheese
Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 20 minutes
