Baked cauliflower
3 starter
Ingredients 6
300 grams cauliflower
75 milliters olive oil
10 grams salt
10 grams pepper
10 grams garlic powder
10 grams onion powder
Steps 4
Wash cauliflower
Mix salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and olive oil
Brush the seasoned oil on the cauliflower
Bake at 150 degrees celsius for 20 minutes

Lasagna
4 main
Ingredients 10
200 grams lasagna sheets
300 grams tomato sauce
150 milliters olive oil
300 grams ground beef
300 grams ground pork
10 grams salt
10 grams pepper
48 grams garlic
48 grams onion
100 grams mozzarella cheese
Steps 7
Boil lasagna sheets for 10 minutes
Mix ground beef and pork with garlic and onion
Sautee the meat in olive oil
Mix in tomato sauce
Season with salt and pepper
Layer the sheets, meat mixture, and cheese
Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 20 minutes
